How It's Made

From Earth
To Your Space

Every StoneCore garden goes through four deliberate stages — each one done slowly, carefully, entirely by hand. Here is what happens before your piece reaches you.

The Process

Four Steps,
One Garden

01
🪨
Stone Selection

We begin at the river. Each stone is hand-picked for its shape, weight, texture, and balance. A stone that doesn't feel right in the hand won't feel right in your garden. We inspect dozens before choosing the ones that make it into a piece. River stones, slate, pebbles — each has a role and each is chosen with intention.

River Stones Slate Natural Pebbles Hand Selected
02
🌿
Natural Elements

Real moss, dried bonsai branches, and preserved greenery are carefully cleaned and treated to last. We use no artificial plants — everything is sourced from nature. The bonsai branches are shaped and trimmed. The moss is preserved so it holds its colour and texture for years without any water or care from you.

Preserved Moss Dried Bonsai Natural Greenery No Artificial Plants
03
🏮
Handcrafting

This is where the garden comes alive. Each miniature shrine, monk figurine, lantern, and bridge is hand-painted with fine brushes and then placed — by eye, by feel — into the composition. There is no template. Every placement decision is made in the moment by the artisan, which is why no two pieces are ever identical. We work slowly. We don't rush this step.

Hand-Painted Miniature Shrines Monk Figurines No Templates
04
📦
Safe Packaging

A Zen garden that arrives broken is no garden at all. Each piece is individually wrapped in soft tissue, secured in custom-cut foam inserts, and placed in a rigid outer box. We test our packaging. Every element stays exactly where the artisan placed it — from our hands to yours, across any distance in India.

Custom Foam Inserts Rigid Box Pan India Delivery 4–7 Days
After Delivery

Caring For Your
Zen Garden

Your StoneCore garden is low-maintenance by design. The preserved moss and dried bonsai need no water or sunlight — just keep them away from a few things and they'll last for years.

Shop Now →
💧
No Water Needed Keep away from direct water and excessive humidity. The preserved elements don't need watering.
☀️
Indirect Light Only Place in indirect sunlight. Direct harsh sun may fade the moss colour over time.
🧹
Gentle Dusting Dust gently with a soft dry cloth or a small brush. Do not use wet cloths or sprays.
📍
Stable Surface Place on a flat, stable surface away from high traffic areas. Avoid spots where it may be knocked over.
