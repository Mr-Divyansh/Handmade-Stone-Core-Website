Ordering & Shipping

Do you ship across India?

+

Yes, we ship to all corners of India! We use trusted courier partners like Delhivery, DTDC, and India Post to ensure your Zen garden reaches you safely. Delivery typically takes 4-7 business days after dispatch.

How much does shipping cost?

+

We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping fee of ₹99 is applicable. Expedited shipping options are available at an additional cost — contact us for details.

How will my order be packaged?

+

Each piece is carefully wrapped in tissue paper, secured with foam inserts, and placed in a rigid double-walled box. We take every precaution to ensure your Zen garden arrives exactly as we made it — not a stone out of place.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Currently, we ship only within India. However, we're working on expanding internationally soon. Follow us on social media for updates on international shipping availability.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

+

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery only if the product arrives damaged or defective. Please photograph the damage immediately upon unboxing and WhatsApp us — we'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Can I cancel my order?

+

Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement. After that, the product may already be in production or dispatched. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp or email to request cancellation.

How long does a refund take?

+

Once we receive and verify the returned product, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method.

Product Care

How do I clean my Zen garden?

+

Dust gently with a soft, dry cloth or a soft-bristled brush. Avoid using water, liquid cleaners, or harsh chemicals. Keep away from direct moisture and excessive humidity.

Are the plants real?

+

Our gardens feature preserved moss and dried bonsai branches — they are real botanical elements but require no watering or maintenance. The moss is specially treated to retain its color and texture for years.

Will the colors fade over time?

+

To preserve the natural colors, place your Zen garden in indirect sunlight. Direct, harsh sunlight may cause fading over extended periods. With proper care, your piece will remain beautiful for years.
